Value-based pricing — the mindset shift
  • 1
    Don't charge for hours. A site that brings 10 new customers/mo at $500 each = $5,000/mo to the client. Why charge $600 because it took you 6 hours?

Build Process

Start to finish — from inquiry to launch

Phase 1 — Discovery
  • 1
    Client intake form — Send before the call. Get: goal, budget, examples they like, content they have.
  • 2
    Discovery call — Listen 80%, talk 20%. Find the real pain. Sell the solution to THAT pain.
  • 3
    Sitemap first. List every page needed. Get client to approve before building — prevents scope creep.
  • 4
    Content first. Use Claude to write all copy before touching design. Words before visuals.
Phase 2 — Build
  • 5
    Build in sections. Header → Hero → About → Services → Social proof → CTA → Footer. One at a time.
  • 6
    Mobile first. Design for 375px wide, scale up. 60%+ of visitors are on phones.
  • 7
    Internal review. Run Lighthouse. Check all links. Test every form. View on real phone.
Phase 3 — Review
  • 8
    Loom walkthrough. Record 3–5 min video walking through every section. Reduces revision confusion.
  • 9
    One feedback channel. Ask for all notes in a single email or doc — not scattered WhatsApp.
  • 10
    Enforce revision limits. 2 rounds in contract. Round 3 = extra charge.
Phase 4 — Launch
  • 11
    Collect final payment before going live. No exceptions. "Site goes live when payment clears."
  • 12
    Set up GA4 + Search Console. Send client a Loom showing where to log in.
  • 13
    Handoff Loom. Record "how to update your site" video. Makes you look pro.

Never promise "Page 1 of Google." Promise "proper SEO setup so Google can find and rank you." That's honest and still valuable.
